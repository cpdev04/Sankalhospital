About Us

Advanced Care, Compassionate Healing.

Sankal Multispeciality Hospital, established in January 2020, is a trusted healthcare provider in Hagaribommanahalli. With 50 beds, expert doctors, and state-of-the-art facilities, we deliver patient- centric, world-class treatments across multiple specialties, ensuring the best clinical outcomes for every patient.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver world-class, patient-centric healthcare with advanced medical facilities, expert care, and a commitment to excellence, ensuring accessible and affordable treatments for holistic well-being.

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading multispecialty hospital in Hagaribommanahalli, setting the standard for excellence in patient care, advanced treatments, and holistic healthcare solutions for the community.
